Software for calculating iES and Rsb statistics following Tang, Thornton & Stoneking, PloS Biology 2007.
by Sebastian E. Ramos-Onsins, version 20190131

Usage:
Tang_stats [Plink_pedigree_modified filename] [number of rows] [number of cols] [threshold value] [seed] [number pops] [col pop1] [col pop2] ... [col pop N]

Otput file is automatically generated with the input filename plus '_Results_Tang.txt'
