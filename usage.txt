Software for calculating iES and Rsb statistics.
following Tang, Thornton & Stoneking, PloS Biology 2007.
verion 20190131

Usage:
Tang_stats [Plink filename] [number of rows] [number of cols] [theshold value] [seed] [number pops] [col pop1] [col pop2] ... [col pop N]

Otput file is automatically generated with the input filename plus '_Results_Tang.txt'
